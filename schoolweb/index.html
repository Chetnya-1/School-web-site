About Our School

Government Senior Secondary School (GSSS), Kakira, is a co-educational, Hindi-medium school located in the Chamba district of Himachal Pradesh.

General Information

  • Location: Kakira village, Chowari block, Chamba district
  • Established: 1937
  • Medium: Hindi
  • Grades: 6th to 12th
  • Management: Department of Education, Government Institution

Infrastructure

  • Campus: Government-owned building with all-weather road access
  • Classrooms: 3 well-maintained instructional rooms
  • Library: Collection of 297 books
  • Computer Lab: 15 functional computers
  • Facilities: Separate toilets for boys and girls, tap water, playground
  • Accessibility: Ramp for students with disabilities, boundary wall

Academic Information

  • Board: Affiliated with Himachal Pradesh State Board (Classes 10 & 12)
  • Admission: Early April for classes XI and XII
  • Session: Academic year begins in April
  • Activities: Participation in academic and extracurricular events
